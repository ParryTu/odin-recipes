Whopper

Description

According to Burger King the "Whopper Sandwich is a Â¼ lb* of savory flame-grilled beef topped with juicy tomatoes, fresh lettuce, creamy mayonnaise, ketchup, crunchy pickles, and sliced white onions on a soft sesame seed bun."

It's not hard to see why this classic so the favorite of so many burger-lovers, including me! Here's how you can make your own Whopper at home and enjoy it anytime:

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Beef Patty
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Keptchup
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Toast buns in a toaster oven for 30s on high heat (optional). Plate buns.
  2. Grill beef patty on medium-high heat on grill until cooked on one side. Flip over and cook the other side until desired rarity.
  3. Plate patty on buns.
  4. Add pickles, lettuce and tomatoes to burger. Chop lettuce and slice tomatoes if not alreadt cut.
  5. Add mayo and ketchup to burger and close with other bun.
  6. Enjoy your Whopper!
